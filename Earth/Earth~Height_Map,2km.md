[Height map](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Height_map "Height map") of planet Earth at 1 Minute = 2km per pixel, including oceanic [bathymetry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bathymetry) information, 
normalized as 8-bit grayscale. 
Because of its easy conversion between x, y pixel information and lat-lon, maps like these are very useful for software map renderings.
